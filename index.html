Diverse Beginnings

Gorillaz is the brainchild of Damon Albarn and Jamie Hewlett. The two met in the 90's and decided to create a "virtual" band with animated characters as avatars in place of themselves. Before the creation of Gorillaz, Damon Albarn was best known as the frontman for the band Blur. At the time, Blur was a massive success in the UK, and well known in the US.

Jamie Hewlett was well known in both the UK and the US at the time as well, for other, more surreal reasons. He is the co-creator of the comic strip Tank Girl, which is about a woman who lives in a dystopian sci-fi future, drives a tank around, and goes on adventures with her boyfriend who is a mutant kangaroo human hybrid (no, seriously, this is all for real).

After hate watching MTV, Albarn and Hewlett came up with the idea for Gorillaz and began to recruit other musicians and flesh out the characters that would make up the band.
